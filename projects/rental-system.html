Rental System

Complete rental management platform with bookings, payments, availability calendar and admin dashboard.

Problem

Short-term rental operators needed a consolidated platform to manage availability, bookings, and payments across multiple listings while providing a smooth booking experience.

Solution

We built a full-stack platform with calendar sync, payment integrations, host dashboards, and role-based access. The front-end was optimized for conversion and mobile-first booking flows.

Impact

  • Reduced booking drop-off by 18%
  • Automated availability reduced double-bookings by 95%
  • Integrated payments improved reconciliation speed by 40%
